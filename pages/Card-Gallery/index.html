Newt Scamander

“Imperfect understanding is often more dangerous than ignorance.”

Newt Scamander cares deeply for his magical creatures. As a Magizoologist, that’s expected, but Newt goes above and beyond for them. When they’re in danger, Newt is the first to leap to their defense. He explains that there is nothing to fear. They will do harm as long as they are left alone and treated well. And Newt is happy to take the blame, the fall, or the punishment so long as his magical creatures are safe. Thus this quote summarizes Newt Scamander in two succinct sentences.

Professor Snape is a master of potions and head of Slytherin House. He is known for his complex character, loyalty to Dumbledore, and critical role in the defeat of Voldemort.

Gellert Grindelwald

Gellert Grindelwald was a dark wizard who sought to establish a new world order in which wizards would rule over non-magical people for the greater good. Driven by a vision of a utopian society, he believed that his cause justified the use of extreme measures, including the use of dark magic and the overthrow of existing institutions. Despite his fall from power, his ideas and actions left a lasting impact on the wizarding world.
